The curtailment trend:

Curtailment is currently limited, at less than 1% of average solar generation. However, network businesses are forecasting curtailment to increase substantially out to 2040 if no action is taken.

Forecast consumer differences: Endeavour Energy

If left unmanaged, some customers will be more affected than others. Generally, the further a solar-equipped home is from the local substation, the more it will experience voltage issues and associated curtailment. As such, fairness becomes a crucial factor in how networks manage curtailment, underlining the need to account for variation among customers. Should every customer have the same level of access to connect and export solar to the grid?
